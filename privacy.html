Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 14, 2025

Data Collection

No personal data required. Fermolog is designed to work without requiring any personal information from you.

  • No account required: You can use Fermolog without creating an account or providing any personal information.
  • Optional email for support only: If you contact us for support, we may collect your email address solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not use this information for marketing purposes.
  • No tracking: We do not track your usage patterns, location, or any other personal data.

App Permissions

Fermolog may request the following permissions on your device:

  • Photos: Optional permission to access your photo library for adding batch images. This permission is only used when you explicitly choose to add photos to your fermentation logs.
  • Notifications: Permission to send you reminders about fermentation steps. You can disable this at any time in your device settings.

Analytics and Tracking

No third-party trackers. Fermolog does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services.

  • We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other tracking tools.
  • We do not collect usage statistics or behavioral data.
  • We do not share any data with third parties.

Data Storage and Retention

Your data stays on your device. All your fermentation logs, notes, and photos are stored locally on your device.

  • Local storage only: Your logs remain on your device unless you explicitly export or share them.
  • No cloud backup: We do not automatically backup your data to any cloud service.
  • Export control: You have full control over when and how you export your data.
  • Deletion: You can delete your data at any time by uninstalling the app or using the app's delete functions.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your data. Fermolog does not sell, rent, or share any personal information with third parties.

  • We do not share data with advertisers.
  • We do not share data with analytics companies.
  • We do not share data with social media platforms.
  • We do not share data with any other third parties.

Children's Privacy

Fermolog is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
